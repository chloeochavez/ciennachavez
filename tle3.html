Lesson 1

In this lesson, I learned about typography and how it is used to improve the appearance and readability of text on a webpage or design. Typography includes choosing the right font style, size, spacing, and arrangement of text. I learned that good typography helps make information easier to read and understand, while also making the design more attractive. It also plays an important role in communicating messages clearly to the audience. Through this lesson, I realized that typography is not only about decoration but also about making designs more effective and organized.

Lesson 2

In this lesson, I learned about text formatting and how it helps make text clearer and more organized on a webpage. Text formatting includes changing the size, style, color, and alignment of text. I realized that formatting is important because it makes information easier to read and understand. It also helps make a webpage look more neat and visually appealing.

Lesson 3

In this lesson, I learned about formatting layout and how it helps organize the content of a webpage. Layout formatting arranges elements like text, images, and sections so the page looks clean and easy to understand. I realized that having a good layout helps users read the content better and makes the webpage more visually appealing.
